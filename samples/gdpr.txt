GENERAL DATA PROTECTION REGULATION (GDPR)
DATA PROCESSING AND PRIVACY NOTICE

This notice explains how Code Phoropter handles your personal data and 
respects your privacy rights under the General Data Protection Regulation 
(EU) 2016/679.

DATA CONTROLLER
Code Phoropter acts as the data controller for any processing activities 
performed through this application. Contact information:
- Website: https://github.com/staffanm/code-phoropter
- Repository: github.com/staffanm/code-phoropter

WHAT DATA WE COLLECT
Code Phoropter is designed with privacy by design principles:

1. NO PERSONAL DATA COLLECTION
   - We do not collect, store, or process any personal information
   - No names, email addresses, or contact details are requested
   - No account creation or registration required

2. TECHNICAL DATA PROCESSED LOCALLY
   - Font preferences and display settings
   - Tournament comparison results  
   - Color scheme selections
   - All processing occurs in your browser only

3. NO COOKIES OR TRACKING
   - No cookies are set on your device
   - No analytics or tracking scripts are used
   - No third-party tracking services integrated
   - No fingerprinting or behavioral tracking

LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING
Where any processing occurs, it is based on:
- Article 6(1)(f) GDPR: Legitimate interests in providing font testing
  functionality
- Article 6(1)(a) GDPR: Your consent when you voluntarily use the 
  application

DATA STORAGE AND RETENTION
- All preference data remains in your browser's local storage
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- Data persists only as long as you choose to keep it
- You can clear all data by clearing your browser storage

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
Limited external requests may be made for:
1. Google Fonts API: Loading web fonts when selected (Google's privacy 
   policy applies)
2. CDN Services: Loading font files from public content delivery networks

These services have their own privacy policies and data processing terms.

YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR
Since we don't collect personal data, most GDPR rights are not applicable. 
However, you retain full control:

- Right to erasure: Clear your browser's local storage
- Right to data portability: Export your settings using the app's export 
  feature
- Right to withdraw consent: Stop using the application at any time

DATA PROTECTION BY DESIGN
Our privacy-first approach includes:
- Client-side processing only
- No external data transmission
- No user accounts or profiles
- No behavioral tracking
- No data monetization
- Open source transparency

CHANGES TO THIS NOTICE
Any changes to this privacy notice will be published in the application's 
repository. As we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify users 
directly of changes.

COMPLAINTS AND CONTACT
For privacy-related concerns:
1. Create an issue at: https://github.com/staffanm/code-phoropter/issues
2. Contact your local data protection authority if you believe your rights 
   have been violated

SUPERVISORY AUTHORITY
You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection 
supervisory authority if you believe we have not complied with GDPR 
requirements.

DATA BREACH PROCEDURE
In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting user data:
1. We will assess the risk to user rights and freedoms
2. Notify relevant supervisory authorities within 72 hours if required
3. Inform affected users without undue delay if there is a high risk

However, since we don't collect or store personal data on our servers, the 
risk of data breaches is minimal.

INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
No personal data is transferred outside the European Union as no personal 
data is collected or stored by our systems.

AUTOMATED DECISION-MAKING
The application uses algorithmic processing for:
- Font similarity calculations
- Tournament scoring
- Display setting optimizations

This processing does not involve personal data and serves only to provide 
better font recommendations based on your preferences.

TECHNICAL AND ORGANIZATIONAL MEASURES
Security measures include:
- Client-side processing eliminates server-side risks
- Open source code allows security auditing
- No authentication systems to compromise
- Minimal external dependencies
- Regular security reviews of dependencies

LAWFUL BASIS DETAILS
Article 6(1)(f) GDPR - Legitimate Interests:
- Our legitimate interest: Providing font testing functionality
- Your interests: Finding optimal coding font settings
- Balance assessment: No personal data collection minimizes privacy impact

DATA MINIMIZATION
We adhere to data minimization principles by:
- Processing only necessary preference data
- Keeping all processing local to your device
- Not storing any data on external systems
- Providing functionality without requiring personal information

CONSENT MANAGEMENT
By using this application, you consent to:
- Local processing of your font preferences
- Temporary loading of fonts from external sources when selected
- Use of browser local storage for saving preferences

You can withdraw consent at any time by:
- Closing the application
- Clearing your browser's local storage
- Using private/incognito browsing mode

This consent is freely given, specific, informed, and can be withdrawn at 
any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent 
before its withdrawal.

EFFECTIVE DATE
This privacy notice is effective from the date of first publication and 
applies to all use of the Code Phoropter application.
